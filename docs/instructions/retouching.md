# Retouching

## Overview

Retouching images in Adobe Photoshop is the skill of enhancing and correcting photos to improve their visual quality while maintaining a natural look. It is a common task people use adobe photoshop for. This skill requires attention to detail, an understanding of composition and color, and the ability to subtly refine images so the final result looks polished and professional without appearing overly edited.
